- Study: Hochrainer et al. 2017
- Source: https://arxiv.org/abs/1610.05529

- Why it matters: strong inverse-problem near miss; ask whether independent coincidence-based momentum widths exist

could become a second no-refit test if independent momentum widths predict visibility profiles
